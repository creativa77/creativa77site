Meet the team

Julian Cerruti

Julian is the founder of Creativa 77. Throughout his 15+ years of experience he has led many challenging, first-of-their-kind software projects to successful completion.

Despite being a successful manager and businessman, Julian's true passion lies is software development and working with technology in general. On his free time, he likes rollerblading, longboarding, biking and working on side projects such as scratch-building R/C airplanes, quadcopters, 3D printers and HTPCs


Tessa Lau

Tessa is a Research Scientist at Willow Garage and a visiting scholar at Creativa 77. She creates simple web-based interfaces for complex personal robots. She knits socks with toes during meetings.


Elvio Toccalino

A software fanatic, Elvio has been chewing code for over five years now. Most of his contributions to Creativa 77 have been developing and extending webserver functionality as well as leveraging the newest web technologies to provide real time operation through the browser. His personal interests include physics, math and music, and he spends his weekends working on side projects.


Sebastián García Marra

Sebastian is an Electronic Engineering student and has been working at Creativa 77 mostly developing and testing ROS-based systems. He's been a robotics enthusiast since he was a kid. He likes software development as well as hardware designing and has experience with different embedded platforms. Currently he's working on a ROS-based robot that could be able to recognize objects, colors and faces. He also works as teaching assistant and takes part of different educational activities in order to spread the word science.
