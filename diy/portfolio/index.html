Our portfolio

Scribe: Real-time transcription by non-expert captionist crowdsourcing

Scribe collects audio from a mobile device and schedules different workers to perform captioning on small audio segments. The distributed captions are then merged into a single text, returning a consolidated transcript to the mobile user.
Our contributions: In this project we play an active role both at the organizational and technical levels. We are responsible for integrating the different services that encompass the application, creating diagnostic and debugging tools and improving the existing systems, both at the back-end and front-end.

Adobe Air Javascript and AJAX Adobe Media Server LAMP Stack Java
