Web developer for service robotics start-up

Creativa 77 in partnership with Savioke is looking for a full-stack Web Developer with experience in server-side JavaScript to participate in the development of a first-of-its-kind service robotics product.

The selected candidate will be part of a globally distributed team with headquarters in Sunnyvale, CA and will be responsible for the development of client- and server-side components of the high-level integration and user interface aspects of the robotics system.

The work environment is fast-paced, iterative and open. Active participation is encouraged and rewarded. The team is a multi-cultural mix of highly-talented engineers and researchers.

Target candidates:

The ideal match for our team is someone who:

  • Loves technology and enjoys getting his/her hands dirty creating technology or dealing with anything that stands in the way.
  • Thrives in a fast-paced environment, working hand-in-hand with smart colleagues on challenging technology problems with ambitious deadlines.
  • Enjoys learning new tools, frameworks, programming languages and paradigms as necessary.
  • Can tackle mid-sized software development tasks independently and deal with ambiguous task assignments.
  • Speaks English fluently enough to work comfortably in an English-speaking environment.

In terms of technology skills the more of these the candidate brings, the better:

  • Experience working the full stack of a Web application.
  • Server-side JavaScript experience, particularly Meteor and Node.js.
  • Front-end JavaScript and frameworks (jquery, foundation, requirejs, underscore, handlebars, socket.io).
  • UNIX/Linux, scripting (bash, python, perl), networking, system administration.
  • Experience working with git, github, jenkins, trello, hipchat, Google Drive, Google Hangouts.
  • ROS, particularly the RobotWebTools project.

We will however consider candidates that don't have experience with these particular technologies but are eager to learn them. We will also take into account general experience, growth potential and cultural match for our candidates.


This position is open to working in the Creativa77's Buenos Aires office or remotely.
