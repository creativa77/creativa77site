Embedded Robotics Software Developer

Creativa 77 in partnership with OSRF and Qualcomm is looking for versatile software developers to become part of the ROS core development team.

The selected candidate will be part of an internationally-recognized globally distributed team with headquarters in Mounatin View, CA and will be responsible for the development of selected ROS features for the Android platform as well as contributing directly to ROS 2.0

The work environment is informal, fast-paced and open. Active participation is encouraged and rewarded. The team is a multi-cultural mix of highly-talented engineers and researchers.

Our candidates

If you love technology and like getting your hands dirty with it, if you thrive in fast-paced environments working with smart colleagues on ambitious deadlines and if you enjoy learning new things as part of your work then chances are you will enjoy working with us.

In terms of necessary skills the more of these you bring, the better:

  • Software development in C++ and Java.
  • UNIX/Linux, scripting (bash, python, perl), build systems, networking, system administration.
  • Development, cross-compilation and debugging of large projects for ARM/Embedded platforms.
  • Development of applications and tools for Android, use of the Android NDK and JNI.
  • Experience with ROS, specifically rosjava.
  • Experience working with tools such as git, github, jenkins, trello, hipchat, slack, etc.
  • Fluency in written and oral English.

We will consider candidates that don't have experience with these particular technologies but are eager to learn them. We will also take into account general experience, growth potential and cultural match for our candidates.


This position is open to working in the Creativa77's Buenos Aires office or for working remotely.
